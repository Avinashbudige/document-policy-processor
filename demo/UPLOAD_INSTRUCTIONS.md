## Pre-Upload Preparation

### 1. Verify Video File

Before uploading, ensure your video meets these requirements:

- [ ] File format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- [ ] Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p) or higher
- [ ] Duration: Under 3 minutes (180 seconds)
- [ ] File size: Under 2 GB (YouTube limit is 256 GB, but smaller is faster)
- [ ] Audio: Clear and audible
- [ ] Quality: No pixelation or artifacts

### 2. Prepare Video Information

Have this information ready before uploading:

**Title**:
```
Document Policy Processor - AI-Powered Insurance Policy Analysis | AWS AI for Bharat Hackathon
```

**Description**:
```
Document Policy Processor is an AI-powered solution that helps users understand which insurance policies apply to their medical situation. Built entirely on AWS, it combines OCR, semantic matching, and large language models to provide instant, accurate policy recommendations.

🔗 GitHub Repository: https://github.com/yourusername/document-policy-processor
🌐 Live Demo: https://your-demo-url.com
📄 Documentation: https://github.com/yourusername/document-policy-processor#readme

🏗️ AWS Services Used:
• AWS Lambda - Serverless compute
• API Gateway - REST API
• Amazon S3 - Document storage
• DynamoDB - Policy database
• AWS Textract - OCR text extraction
• CloudWatch - Monitoring and logging

🚀 Key Features:
• Upload medical documents (PDF, PNG, JPG, TXT)
• AI-powered text extraction with AWS Textract
• Semantic policy matching using embeddings
• LLM-based exclusion checking with GPT-3.5

🎯 Built for: AWS AI for Bharat Hackathon 2025

#AWS #AI #MachineLearning #Hackathon #InsurTech #DocumentProcessing #NLP #OCR
```

- Use high contrast and readable text
- Save as JPG or PNG

## YouTube Account Setup

### 1. Create or Sign In to YouTube Account

**If you don't have a YouTube account**:
1. Go to https://www.youtube.com/
2. Click "Sign In" in top right
3. Click "Create account"
4. Follow the prompts to create a Google account
5. Verify your email address

**If you have a YouTube account**:
1. Go to https://www.youtube.com/
2. Click "Sign In" in top right
3. Enter your credentials

### 2. Verify Your Account (Required for Videos Over 15 Minutes)

Your video is under 3 minutes, so verification is optional but recommended:

1. Go to https://www.youtube.com/verify
2. Select your country
3. Choose verification method (text message or voice call)
4. Enter your phone number
5. Enter the verification code you receive
6. Click "Submit"

**Note**: Verification allows you to:
- Upload videos longer than 15 minutes
- Add custom thumbnails
- Live stream
- Appeal Content ID claims

## Upload Process

### Step 1: Start Upload

1. Go to https://www.youtube.com/
2. Click the camera icon (➕) in top right
3. Select "Upload video"
4. Or go directly to: https://studio.youtube.com/

### Step 2: Select File

1. Click "SELECT FILES" button
2. Navigate to your video file
3. Select `Document_Policy_Processor_Demo_Final.mp4`
4. Click "Open"

**Upload will begin automatically**

### Step 3: Video Details

While the video uploads, fill in the details:

**Title**:
```
Document Policy Processor - AI-Powered Insurance Policy Analysis | AWS AI for Bharat Hackathon
```

**Description**:
Paste the description prepared earlier (see Pre-Upload Preparation section)

**Thumbnail**:
- Click "Upload thumbnail"
- Select your custom thumbnail image (if you created one)
- Or use auto-generated thumbnail

**Playlist** (optional):
- Click "Select" under Playlists
- Create new playlist: "AWS Hackathon Projects"
- Or skip this step

**Audience**:
- Select "No, it's not made for kids"
- (Your video is technical content for adults)

**Age Restriction**:
- Select "No" (your content is appropriate for all ages)

### Step 4: Video Elements (Optional)

**End Screen**:
- Click "Add" to add end screen
- Add elements like "Subscribe" button or link to GitHub
- Or skip for now (can add later)

**Cards**:
- Click "Add card"
- Add link to GitHub repository or live demo
- Or skip for now (can add later)

### Step 5: Checks

YouTube will automatically check your video:

- [ ] Copyright - Should show "No issues found"
- [ ] Ad suitability - Should show "Suitable for most advertisers"

If there are issues:
- Review the specific issue
- Make corrections if needed
- Or proceed if issues are minor

### Step 6: Visibility

**Choose visibility setting**:

**Option 1: Unlisted (Recommended for Hackathon)**
- ✅ Anyone with the link can view
- ✅ Won't appear in search results or your channel
- ✅ Perfect for sharing with judges
- ✅ Can change to Public later

**Option 2: Public**
- Anyone can find and view
- Will appear in search results
- Good for showcasing your work
- Can attract more views

**Option 3: Private**
- Only you and people you choose can view
- Must add specific email addresses
- Not recommended for hackathon (judges may not have access)

**Recommendation**: Choose "Unlisted" for hackathon submission

### Step 7: Publish

1. Review all settings
2. Click "PUBLISH" button
3. Wait for processing to complete (1-5 minutes)
4. Copy the video URL

## Video Settings

### After Publishing

You can adjust settings after publishing:

1. Go to https://studio.youtube.com/
2. Click "Content" in left sidebar
3. Find your video and click on it
4. Click "Details" to edit information

### Recommended Settings

**Basic Info**:
- Title: Clear and descriptive
- Description: Complete with links
- Tags: Relevant keywords

**Advanced Settings**:
- Category: "Science & Technology"
- Comments: "Allow all comments" or "Hold potentially inappropriate comments for review"
- Ratings: "Users can view ratings for this video"
- License: "Standard YouTube License"

**Subtitles/CC** (optional but recommended):
- YouTube auto-generates captions
- Review and edit for accuracy
- Or upload your own SRT file

## Privacy Settings

### Unlisted Video Settings

If you chose "Unlisted":

- [ ] Video is not searchable
- [ ] Only people with link can view

- [ ] Can be embedded on websites

**To get the link**:

You can change privacy settings anytime:

1. Go to YouTube Studio

3. Find your video

6. Click "SAVE"

## Getting Your Video URL

### Standard URL Format

Your video URL will look like:
```
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID
```

Example:
```
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
```

### Short URL Format

YouTube also provides a short URL:
```
https://youtu.be/VIDEO_ID
```

Example:
```
https://youtu.be/dQw4w9WgXcQ
```

**Both formats work - use whichever you prefer**

### To Copy Your URL

**Method 1: From Video Page**
1. Go to your video on YouTube
2. Click "SHARE" button below video
3. Click "COPY" to copy the link

**Method 2: From YouTube Studio**
1. Go to https://studio.youtube.com/
2. Click "Content"
3. Find your video
4. Hover over the title
5. Click the link icon to copy URL

## Alternative Platforms

If you prefer not to use YouTube, here are alternatives:

### Vimeo

**Pros**: Professional, clean interface, no ads
**Cons**: Free tier has upload limits

**Upload Process**:
1. Create account at https://vimeo.com/
2. Click "Upload" button
3. Select your video file
4. Fill in title and description
5. Choose privacy: "Unlisted" or "Public"
6. Click "Save"

**Free Tier Limits**:
- 500 MB per week upload limit
- 5 GB total storage

### Google Drive

**Pros**: Easy sharing, no processing time, large storage
**Cons**: Not a video platform, less professional

**Upload Process**:
1. Go to https://drive.google.com/
2. Click "New" → "File upload"
3. Select your video file
4. Right-click on uploaded file
5. Click "Share"
6. Change to "Anyone with the link"
7. Copy the link

### Loom

**Pros**: Easy to use, designed for demos
**Cons**: Watermark on free tier, 5-minute limit on free tier

**Note**: Your video is under 3 minutes, so this works

**Upload Process**:
1. Go to https://www.loom.com/
2. Sign up for free account
3. Click "Upload video"
4. Select your video file
5. Wait for processing
6. Copy the share link

## Troubleshooting

### Upload Fails

**Problem**: Upload stops or fails
- **Solution**: Check internet connection
- **Solution**: Try a different browser
- **Solution**: Reduce video file size
- **Solution**: Try uploading at a different time

### Video Stuck Processing

**Problem**: Video processing takes too long (>30 minutes)
- **Solution**: Wait - processing can take time for HD videos
- **Solution**: Check YouTube Studio for processing status
- **Solution**: If stuck for hours, delete and re-upload

### Video Quality is Poor

**Problem**: Video looks pixelated or blurry
- **Solution**: Wait for HD processing to complete (can take 30+ minutes)
- **Solution**: Ensure you uploaded 1080p video
- **Solution**: Re-export video at higher bitrate

### Can't Add Custom Thumbnail

**Problem**: Thumbnail upload option is grayed out
- **Solution**: Verify your YouTube account (see Account Setup section)
- **Solution**: Wait 24 hours after verification

### Video is Blocked

**Problem**: YouTube flags video for copyright or other issues
- **Solution**: Review the specific issue in YouTube Studio
- **Solution**: Ensure you have rights to all content (music, images)
- **Solution**: Appeal if you believe it's a mistake
